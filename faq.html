Instructions to view the Aeron (ARN) and CryptoBonusMiles (CBM) tokens


  • What are the ARN token smart contract details?
  • Contract address: 0xBA5F11b16B155792Cf3B2E6880E8706859A8AEB6

  • Token name: Aeron

  • Token symbol: ARN

  • Decimals: 8


  • What are the CBM token smart contract details?
  • Contract address: 0x95eFD1Fe6099F65a7ED524DEF487483221094947

  • Token name: CryptoBonusMiles

  • Token symbol: CBM

  • Decimals: 18


  • ERC20 compatible wallets to receive your tokens
  • The following wallets are known to be compatible with Ethereum tokens (ERC20 standard) and may allow you to receive and view your token balances: MyEtherWallet (no download needed), Mist (Desktop), Parity (Desktop), imToken (Mobile), Metamask (Browser plugin).

  • Do the tokens load automatically or do I have to input some parameters?
  • MyEtherWallet (MEW) and some other popular wallets have integrated ARN and CBM tokens natively and the tokens will load automatically. In other wallets, to discover a token you may need to select "Contract" and/or "Standard ERC20 token" and input Contract address, Token symbol and Decimals in the respective fields. Please refer to the user instructions for your particular wallet.

  • Is ARN token listed at CoinMarketCap?
  • Please follow the link to view token details and active markets: Aeron (ARN) token.
